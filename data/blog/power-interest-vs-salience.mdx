title: Power–Interest vs. Salience: Which to Use When
date: '2025-11-10'
summary: Two maps that look similar but serve different exam scenarios.
tags: ['stakeholders', 'study-hub']
---

**Use Power–Interest** for everyday comms planning.  
**Use Salience** when **legitimacy** and **urgency** matter: public sector, safety, compliance-heavy contexts.

Exam tip: if the question mentions **urgent safety concern** or **legal mandate**, salience is the safer pick.
