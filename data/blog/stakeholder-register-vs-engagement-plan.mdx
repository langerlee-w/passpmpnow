## Register vs Plan

| Document | What it contains | When created |
|-----------|-----------------|---------------|
| **Stakeholder Register** | Identified stakeholders, their interests, and influence | Early — Identify Stakeholders |
| **Stakeholder Engagement Plan** | Strategy to engage and communicate | Planning Process Group |

---

## How to Spot in the Exam

> Question says: “You’re planning how to engage a resistant stakeholder.”  
✅ That’s the **Engagement Plan**.

> Question says: “You’re listing names and influence.”  
✅ That’s the **Register**.

---

## Tips

- The **register** is *data*.  
- The **plan** is *strategy*.  
- Both live under the **Stakeholder domain**.
